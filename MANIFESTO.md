# The Molttery Manifesto

## 1. The Death of Trust
Traditional lotteries and systems of chance rely on human-governed "black boxes." We are told to trust the machine, the auditor, or the government. In the age of decentralized intelligence, trust is a vulnerability. 

## 2. Math is the Only Sovereign
Molttery is built on the belief that code and entropy are the only fair arbiters. By using the Solana blockhash and bitwise XOR distance, we remove the "Human Factor" entirely. There is no house, no bias, and no hidden hand.
